Seminar on Probability: Outlook and Open Notions (SPOON)

A bi-weekly seminar for PhD students and postdocs in probability at the University of Münster. Topics for discussion are works in progress, interesting topics you have come across, or that arxiv paper you are reading and want to understand better. The goal is that we tell each other about our work, exchange and have discussions in a relaxed atmosphere.

The seminar takes place at 11:00 on room SRZ 214.
Organizer:
Tara Trauthwein. Contact if you would like to give a talk!
