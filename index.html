PhD student at Département de Mathématiques et Applications (DMA) of École Normale Supérieure (Paris). My advisor is Anna Erschler.

My main interests are symbolic dynamics on groups, and the geometric properties of Cayley graphs of finitely generated groups. I try to understand things that depend on the choice of generating set, as for example the existence of dead ends of unbounded depth, or growth series.
