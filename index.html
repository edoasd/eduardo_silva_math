Eduardo Silva
I am a PhD student at École Normale Supérieure (Paris) under the supervision of Anna Erschler.

Before, I did a Mathematical Engineering degree together with a Master in Applied Mathematics at Universidad de Chile, Master thesis under the direction of Michael Schraudner,
and a Master 2 Mathématiques Fondamentales at Université Sorbonne Paris Nord, Master mémoire under the direction of Romain Tessera.

My main interests are Symbolic Dynamics on groups and Geometric Group Theory, in particular the growth of groups and the geometric properties of their Cayley graphs.
